This PDF is about decreasing the amount of discrimination towards disabled bodies. It was a study done where employees were put into three groups, a control, education, or contact group and had an intervention where they were shown videos to increase their knowledge. After the experiment, it was found that for a very short period of time, discrimination was reduced, however did not last long. This was due to the fact that the information from the videos was just learned, not retained. So, if employers made employees watch interactive videos to confirm they understand, discrimination can decrease. Project WHEN

Project WHEN is all about ending workplace harassment. Under this project, one of their goals is to stop disability discrimination in workplaces. Their mission as a whole is to elevate awareness to eliminate harassment and create more respectful workplaces. The project started in 2016 with many personal stories and court cases of discrimination occuring in the state of Ohio. With any cause citizens were fed up and tired of receiving this treatment. With generous donations the group was able to start up and hold monthly virtual and in person meetings discussing new news on the topic, legislation, and national court cases. Based on the website the group is very close and many of them have experienced some level of discrimination on the job. Many of the members live in the same area and work in the same factory. The group was nationally recognized for thier efforts in Washington D.C. at the start of 2020. They recieved multiple awards for there time and efforts spent bringing this issue to light nationally. The group plans to keep spreading the word and nationally recongize this issue. So far they have reached outposts in 33 states and plan to continue this journey. The twitter is linked below which has even more in depth information about the organization. Even with the pandemic they are active and reaching out to victims across the country and world. If you go onto their website, projectwhen.org, you will find places to donate, and many ways to stop and prevent discrimination. Our group felt that this group had the right approach when going about this issue. There are multiple others working towards a common goal, but through education and peace this group is acheiving sucess. Many others are using a scare technique which is effective, but not something our group stands for. Project When is a great group fighting against this common issue in the United States.
